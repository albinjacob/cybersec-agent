This document describes our security posture in plain prose without any markdown heading
markers at all. We expect all employees to use strong passwords and enable multi-factor
authentication wherever it is offered. Servers should be patched promptly after a vendor
releases a security update, and firewall rules should default to deny unless a specific
exception has been approved by the security team. This paragraph exists to confirm the
policy chunker's heading-based splitter handles a document with no "## " markers gracefully,
without crashing, rather than to test any specific compliance mapping.
